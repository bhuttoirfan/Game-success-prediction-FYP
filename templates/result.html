About Games & Research
Video games are one of the most popular sources of entertainment within our society. With the evolution of science and technology the demand of better-quality contents with respect to improved graphics and more real-life experience have increased for video games. Such demand from users has led to high production cost, ultimately leading to higher business risk for the development companies. In order to minimize this risk this research proposes a machine learning approach towards video game success before the initial release.

Our Research will benefit Game Development Companies to have better understanding of gaming community and How the trend in shifting over the years. In early, Arcades games used to be the most played games in the world but over the time the community have shifted towards more competitive and Strategic games. The project will give an idea of today’s trends and predict the possibility of a game becoming a success via the trained model.
